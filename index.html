About Us

Paglaom Community Radio 96.5 FM is a community-based radio station broadcasting from Cabitan, Mandaon, Masbate, Philippines.

Our mission is to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ and share the Good News of salvation to all listeners through uplifting Christian music, inspirational messages, and community programs.

We are committed to serving our community by providing hope, encouragement, and spiritual guidance through quality broadcasting that honors God and uplifts the human spirit.
